Question

(photo) Choose the statement that best describes the picture.

Choices

  1. (A) A woman is standing in front of a shelf of books in a library.
  2. (B) A family is sitting on a sofa and watching television in a living room.
  3. (C) A man is cleaning windows on the outside of a building.
  4. (D) Some workers are unloading boxes from a truck.
Correct answer: B
Explanation: The scene takes place in a living room with a family watching TV. The other statements show library, outdoor cleaning, or delivery work.
