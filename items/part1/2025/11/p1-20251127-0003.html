Question

(photo) Choose the statement that best describes the picture.

Choices

  1. (A) A man is taking money from an ATM machine.
  2. (B) A child is feeding ducks at a pond.
  3. (C) A worker is stacking boxes on a shelf.
  4. (D) A receptionist is speaking with a visitor at a front desk.
Correct answer: D
Explanation: The correct sentence describes a receptionist and a visitor at an office desk. The others are not reception scenes.
