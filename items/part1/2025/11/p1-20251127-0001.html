Question

(photo) Choose the statement that best describes the picture.

Choices

  1. (A) A man is repairing a computer monitor on a desk.
  2. (B) Some people are jogging along a riverside path.
  3. (C) A child is standing on a stool and helping an adult bake cookies.
  4. (D) A woman is selling tickets at a theater window.
Correct answer: C
Explanation: Only the third sentence describes a child helping with baking in a kitchen. The other options show different activities.
